Mission: Make Delhi carbon neutral by 2040

We are committed to accelerating Delhi's transition to a carbon neutral future through scalable biochar systems, farmer-first partnerships, and verifiable carbon removal.

From diverting urban biomass waste to reinvigorating soils across the capital region, Biochar By Madhu delivers measurable climate action that keeps Delhi's air clean and communities thriving.

Mission-driven climate action

Biochar By Madhu exists to bridge the gap between global climate commitments and local realities. We design transparent, measurable carbon removal projects that improve livelihoods, regenerate degraded lands, and deliver climate wins that last for generations.

Integrity

Our Approach

A full-stack platform for nature-positive carbon removal

Project origination

We identify agricultural and forestry waste streams, co-develop projects with cooperatives, and provide the technology required for clean pyrolysis and biochar production.

MRV intelligence

Remote sensing, field analytics, and IoT sensors capture real-time data to quantify carbon removal and agronomic benefits.

Premium carbon credits

Verified credits meet the highest standards, connecting climate investors with measurable social and environmental impact.
