Flagship Initiatives

Scaling regenerative systems across India

Punjab Biochar Alliance

Converting paddy straw into biochar to eliminate seasonal stubble burning while supporting farmer-led pyrolysis cooperatives.

  • 8,500+ farmers engaged
  • 40 kilns deployed
  • Verra-aligned methodology

Deccan Drylands Revival

Restoring degraded soils with agroforestry waste biochar, drip irrigation, and climate-smart crop diversification.

  • 22,000 hectares under regeneration
  • Public-private financing model
  • Community-led monitoring network

North-East Bamboo Circularity

Harnessing bamboo residues for carbon removal, clean energy, and new material innovations in tribal communities.

  • 50,000 tonnes biomass redirected
  • On-site renewable power generation
  • Inclusive revenue sharing
